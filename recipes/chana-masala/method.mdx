#### Curry
- Add a can of chopped tomatoes to the paste along with two drained cans of chickpeas
- Cut up 100g of creamed coconut into smaller pieces and add with a little water
- Simmer on a low heat for at least 25 minutes, stirring occasionally (longer if you have time)
- Stir in the spinach until wilted and squeeze in quarter of a lemon before serving

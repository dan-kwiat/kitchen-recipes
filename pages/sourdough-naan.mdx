# Sourdough Naan

<Badges
  quantity='makes 8 naans'
  time='20m work, 3h total'
/>

---
## Ingredients

- [ ] 150g strong white flour
- [ ] 150g strong wholewheat flour
- [ ] 150g natural yogurt
- [ ] 150g ripe sourdough starter
- [ ] 50ml water
- [ ] 1 teaspoon baking powder
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] Butter

---
## Method

#### Preparation

- [ ] Mix all the ingredients except for the butter in a bowl
- [ ] Tip the dough onto a surface and knead for a few minutes
- [ ] Put it back in the bowl and cover with a tea towel
- [ ] Set a timer for 3 hours

#### After resting

- [ ] Divide into 8 pieces
- [ ] Heat a heavy frying pan
- [ ] Melt some butter in a bowl

#### For each piece

- [ ] Roll to 5mm thickness
- [ ] Brush the top side with butter
- [ ] Place top side down in the pan and brush the other side
- [ ] Cook for 1 minute on each side

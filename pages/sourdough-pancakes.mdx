# Sourdough Pancakes
#### Thick and fluffy, American style

<Badges
  quantity='4 pancakes'
  time='15m'
/>

---
## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 cup (130g) plain flour
- [ ] 1 cup (180g) sourdough starter
- [ ] 1.5 teaspoon baking powder
- [ ] 0.5 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
- [ ] 1 tablespoon sugar
- [ ] 0.5 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 0.5 cup (120ml) milk (plant-based is fine)
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 2 tablespoons oil
- [ ] Butter or oil

---
## Method

- [ ] Beat the egg in a mixing bowl then add the milk and starter
- [ ] Mix in the flour, sugar, salt, baking powder and bicarbonate of soda
- [ ] Add the oil to the batter
- [ ] Put a frying pan on medium-high heat
- [ ] Melt a knob of butter in the pan
- [ ] Ladle some batter into the pan, making a 10cm diameter pancake
- [ ] Cook one side then flip with a spatula to do the other

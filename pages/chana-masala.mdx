# Chana Masala

<Badges
  quantity='4 servings'
  time='45m'
/>

---
## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 1 teaspoon fresh or dried chilli, to taste
- [ ] 9 garlic cloves (~1 small bulb)
- [ ] Thumb-sized piece of ginger
- [ ] 1 tablespoon ground coriander
- [ ] 1 tablespoon garam masala
- [ ] 2 tablespoons ground cumin
- [ ] 2 tablespoons tomato purée
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] Ghee, butter or oil
- [ ] 2 cans chickpeas
- [ ] 1 can chopped tomatoes
- [ ] 100g creamed coconut
- [ ] 100g spinach
- [ ] Quarter lemon

#### Optional accompaniments

- [ ] Fresh coriander
- [ ] Yogurt
- [ ] Basmati Rice
- [ ] Dal
- [ ] [Sourdough Naan](./sourdough-naan)

---
## Method

#### Paste
- [ ] Heat some ghee in a large pot
- [ ] Finely dice the onion and add to the pot with 1 teaspoon of fresh or dried chilli
- [ ] Grate the <Aka terms={['9 cloves']}>garlic</Aka> and <Aka terms={['Thumb-sized piece']}>ginger</Aka>, and add to the pan once the onions have softened
- [ ] Add the <Aka terms={['1 coriander + 1 garam masala + 2 cumin (tablespoons)']}>spices</Aka>, <Aka terms={['2 tablespoons']}>tomato purée</Aka> and <Aka terms={['1 teaspoon']}>salt</Aka>
- [ ] Add a drop of water or oil if needed
- [ ] Cook for another 2 mins over a medium-high heat, stirring occasionally

#### Rice

- [ ] If you're doing rice alongside, now is a good time to start

#### Curry
- [ ] Add a can of chopped tomatoes to the paste along with two drained cans of chickpeas
- [ ] Cut up 100g of creamed coconut into smaller pieces and add with a little water
- [ ] Simmer on a low heat for at least 25 minutes, stirring occasionally (longer if you have time)
- [ ] Stir in the spinach until wilted and squeeze in quarter of a lemon before serving
